The UN High Commissioner for Refugees, Filippo Grandi, has stated that the humanitarian consequences of this conflict will be catastrophic for civilians. Years of conflict along the eastern border have already displaced 1.3 million people and killed 14,000 people. With your help, we hope to provide emergency relief and recovery to the homeless. In Ukraine and neighbouring countries, UNHCR has increased its operations and capacity. We remain steadfast in our commitment to assisting all affected populations in Ukraine and neighbouring countries. Your assistance can help ensure that people fleeing Ukraine are safe and secure.
